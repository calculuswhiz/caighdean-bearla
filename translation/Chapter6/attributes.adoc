:chapterTitle_en: The Adverb

:6-1-title_ga: Príomhchineálacha Dobhriathra
:6-1-title_en: Main Types of Adverbs

:6-1-1-p1_ga: Úsáidtear an dobhriathar mar cháiliú ar bhriathar, ainm briathartha, aidiacht, réamhfhocal, cónasc nó dobhriathar eile.
:6-1-1-p1_en: The adverb is used to qualify a verb, verbal noun, adjective, preposition, conjunction or another adverb.

:6-1-1-p2_ga: Seo a leanas na príomhchineálacha dobhriathra de réir feidhme --
:6-1-1-p2_en: The following are the main types of adverbs according to function --

:6-1-1-a-p_ga: *dobhriathar modha*, e.g., _amhlaidh_, _ar chor ar bith_, _go breá_, _go dona_, _go hard_, _freisin_, _os íseal_:
:6-1-1-a-p_en: *adverb of manner*, e.g., _amhlaidh_, _ar chor ar bith_, _go breá_, _go dona_, _go hard_, _freisin_, _os íseal_:

:6-1-1-a-SampleBox-eg1_ga: Shiúil sí *go mall*.
:6-1-1-a-SampleBox-eg1_en: She walked *slowly*.

:6-1-1-a-SampleBox-eg2_ga: Tá sí á rá *os íseal*.
:6-1-1-a-SampleBox-eg2_en: She is saying it *lowly*. (I.e. whispering)

:6-1-1-b-p_ga: *dobhriathar ama agus minicíochta*, e.g., _amárach_, _anois_, _arís_, _arú inné_, _choíche_, _Dé Sathairn_, _go hannamh_, _go minic_, _go míosúil_, _inné_, _riamh_:
:6-1-1-b-p_en: *adverbs of time and frequency*, e.g., _amárach_, _anois_, _arís_, _arú inné_, _choíche_, _Dé Sathairn_, _go hannamh_, _go minic_, _go míosúil_, _inné_, _riamh_:

:6-1-1-b-SampleBox-eg1_ga: Beidh sí anseo *amárach*.
:6-1-1-b-SampleBox-eg1_en: She will be here *tomorrow*.

:6-1-1-b-SampleBox-eg2_ga: Is *annamh* a fheicim anois iad.
:6-1-1-b-SampleBox-eg2_en: I *rarely* see them now.

:6-1-1-c-p_ga: *dobhriathar áite agus treo*, e.g., _abhaile_, _abhus_, _amach_, _amú_, _amuigh_, _ann_, _anseo_, _ansin_, _isteach_, _ó thuaidh_, _siar_, _soir_:
:6-1-1-c-p_en: *adverbs of place and direction*, e.g., _abhaile_, _abhus_, _amach_, _amú_, _amuigh_, _ann_, _anseo_, _ansin_, _isteach_, _ó thuaidh_, _siar_, _soir_:

:6-1-1-c-SampleBox-eg1_ga: Ní fhaca mé *ann* í.
:6-1-1-c-SampleBox-eg1_en: I didn't see her *there*.

:6-1-1-c-SampleBox-eg2_ga: D'imigh sé *amach*.
:6-1-1-c-SampleBox-eg2_en: He went *out*.

:6-1-1-d-p_ga: *dobhriathar ceisteach* (cé gur forainmnigh cheisteacha iad na míreanna seo (féach 8.5), is minic a dhéantar tagairt dóibh mar dhobhriathra ceisteacha), e.g., _cá_ (_cá bhfuil_, _cá háit_), _cad_ (_cad a…_, _cad as_, _cad chuige_, _cad é_, _cad é mar_), _cathain_ (_cathain a…_), _céard_ (_céard a…_, _céard é_), _cén_ (_cén chaoi_, _cén uair_), _conas_ (_conas a…_):
:6-1-1-d-p_en: *interrogative adverb* (although these clauses are interrogative pronouns (see 8.5), they are often referred to as interrogative adverbs), e.g., _cá_ (_cá bhfuil_, _cá háit_), _cad_ (_cad a…_, _cad as_, _cad chuige_, _cad é_, _cad é mar_), _cathain_ (_cathain a…_), _céard_ (_céard a…_, _céard é_), _cén_ (_cén chaoi_, _cén uair_), _conas_ (_conas a…_):

:6-1-1-d-SampleBox-eg1_ga: *Cá* rachaimid?
:6-1-1-d-SampleBox-eg1_en: *Where* shall we go?

:6-1-1-d-SampleBox-eg2_ga: *Cén chaoi* a bhfuil tú?
:6-1-1-d-SampleBox-eg2_en: *How* are you?

:6-1-1-e-p_ga: *dobhriathar cainníochta*, e.g., _ar fad_, _beagnach_, _sách_:
:6-1-1-e-p_en: *adverb of quantity*, e.g., _ar fad_, _beagnach_, _sách_:

:6-1-1-e-SampleBox-eg1_ga: Níl sé *sách* te fós.
:6-1-1-e-SampleBox-eg1_en: It's not *quite* hot yet.

:6-1-1-e-SampleBox-eg2_ga: Bhí sibh *beagnach* in am.
:6-1-1-e-SampleBox-eg2_en: You were *almost* on time.

:6-1-1-f-p1_ga: *dobhriathar breischéime*, e.g., _níos_, _ní ba_:
:6-1-1-f-p1_en: *adverb of comparative degree*, e.g., _níos_, _ní ba_:

:6-1-1-f-SampleBox-eg1_ga: Tá Seán *níos* airde ná Máire.
:6-1-1-f-SampleBox-eg1_en: Seán is tall**er** than Mary.

:6-1-1-f-SampleBox-eg2_ga: Bhí siad *ní ba* ghile ná an sneachta.
:6-1-1-f-SampleBox-eg2_en: They were whit**er** than snow.

:6-1-1-f-p2_ga: Tabhair faoi deara gur féidir _níos_ a úsáid sna haimsirí go léir ach nach n-úsáidtear _ní b'_/_ní ba_ ach san aimsir chaite, san aimsir ghnáthchaite agus sa mhodh coinníollach. Déantar _ní b' a úsáid roimh aidiacht dar tús guta nó _fh-_ + _guta_, e.g., _Ba ghá dóibh dréimire níos fearr_/_ní b'_fhearr a fháil anuraidh_; _Dá mbeinn bliain níos óige_/_ní b'óige_, _d'imreoinn an cluiche_; agus déantar _ní ba_ a úsáid roimh chonsan, e.g., _Bhí na braillíní níos gile_/_ní ba ghile ná an sneachta_.
:6-1-1-f-p2_en: Note that _níos_ can be used in all tenses but _ní b'_/_ní ba_ is only used in the past tense, the past habitual tense and the conditional mood. _ní b'_ is used before an adjective beginning with a vowel or _fh-_ + _vowel_, e.g., _Ba ghá dóibh dréimire níos fearr_/_ní b'fhearr a fháil anuraidh_; _Dá mbeinn bliain níos óige_/_ní b'óige_, _d'imreoinn an cluiche_; and _ní ba_ is usedbefore a consonant, e.g., _Bhí na braillíní níos gile_/_ní ba ghile ná an sneachta_.

:6-1-1-g-p_ga: *dobhriathar treise*, e.g., _féin_:
:6-1-1-g-p_en: *strong adverb*, e.g., _féin_:

:6-1-1-g-SampleBox-eg1_ga: Má bhí sé tuirseach *féin*, chuaigh sé chuig an Oireachtas.
:6-1-1-g-SampleBox-eg1_en: If he was tired *himself*, he went to the Oireachtas.

:6-1-1-g-SampleBox-eg2_ga: Is ócáid thábhachtach í; beidh an tUachtarán *féin* ann.
:6-1-1-g-SampleBox-eg2_en: It is an important event; the President *himself* will be there.

:6-1-1-h-p_ga: *ainmfhocal a úsáid go dobhriathartha i gcúrsaí tomhais*, e.g., _ar leithead_; _ar fad_:
:6-1-1-h-p_en: *noun used as an adverb in measurement*, e.g., _ar leithead_; _ar fad_:

:6-1-1-h-SampleBox-eg1_ga: Bhí an seomra suite sé mhéadar *ar fad*.
:6-1-1-h-SampleBox-eg1_en: The room was located six meters *in length*.

:6-1-1-h-SampleBox-eg2_ga: Tá an bóithrín trí mhéadar *ar leithead*.
:6-1-1-h-SampleBox-eg2_en: The road is three meters *in width*.

:6-1-1-i-p_ga: *aidiacht a úsáid go dobhriathartha*, e.g., _gann_, _gearr_:
:6-1-1-i-p_en: *adjective used as an adverb*, e.g., _gann_, _gearr_:

:6-1-1-i-SampleBox-eg1_ga: Bhí siad duine *gearr*.
:6-1-1-i-SampleBox-eg1_en: They were a *short* person.

:6-1-1-i-SampleBox-eg2_ga: Bhí muid *gann* i mbainne.
:6-1-1-i-SampleBox-eg2_en: We were *short* of milk.

:6-1-1-j-p_ga: *dobhriathar a úsáid go haidiachtach*, e.g., _amach_, _aniar_, _anuas_, _isteach_:
:6-1-1-j-p_en: *adverb used as an adjective*, e.g., _amach_, _aniar_, _anuas_, _isteach_: *TL Note: Similar constructions do not exist in English. These imply direction or origin. E.g. a literal translation of "fear amach" would be "outwardly man", but that is not idiomatic English. Translations here will just give the meaning without attempting to parallel.*

:6-1-1-j-SampleBox-eg1_ga: An bhfeiceann tú an fear *amach*?
:6-1-1-j-SampleBox-eg1_en: Do you see the man who went out?

:6-1-1-j-SampleBox-eg2_ga: Seo chugainn an bhean *isteach*.
:6-1-1-j-SampleBox-eg2_en: Here comes the woman who came in.

:6-1-1-j-SampleBox-eg3_ga: Tá an bhean *aniar* ar an teilifís.
:6-1-1-j-SampleBox-eg3_en: The woman from the west is on TV.

:6-1-1-j-SampleBox-eg4_ga: Tá braon *anuas* sa seomra codlata.
:6-1-1-j-SampleBox-eg4_en: There is a seepage from above in the bedroom.

:6-1-1-j-SampleBox-eg5_ga: Is é Tomás an cliamhain *isteach*.
:6-1-1-j-SampleBox-eg5_en: Tomás is the man that married into the household.

:6-1-1-j-SampleBox-eg6_ga: Tá an ghaoth *anoir* is an fhearthainn *aniar* air.
:6-1-1-j-SampleBox-eg6_en: The wind from the east tonight tonight and the rain is from the west.

:6-1-1-k-p_ga: *ainmfhocal nó ainm briathartha atá bunaithe ar dhobhriathar*, e.g., _ganfhiosaíocht_, _istoícheadóireacht_:
:6-1-1-k-p_en: *a noun or a verb based on an adverb*, e.g., _ganfhiosaíocht_, _istoícheadóireacht_:

:6-1-1-k-SampleBox-eg1_ga: Tá an bleachtaire *ag ganfhiosaíocht* ar gach duine.
:6-1-1-k-SampleBox-eg1_en: The detective is *acting surreptitiously* to everyone. (*ganfhiosach* means "secret")

:6-1-1-k-SampleBox-eg2_ga: Tá an iomarca *istoícheadóireachta* ar siúl san áit seo.
:6-1-1-k-SampleBox-eg2_en: There is too much *night-activity* going on in this place. (*istoíche* - "at night" + *-adóireachta* - "activities")
