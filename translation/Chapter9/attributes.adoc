:chapterTitle_ga: An Uimhir
:chapterTitle_en: The Number

:9-1-title_ga: Ginearálta
:9-1-title_en: General

:9-1-1-p_ga: Is iad na príomhleaganacha den uimhir sa Ghaeilge an Mhaoluimhir, an Bhunuimhir, an Uimhir Phearsanta, an Orduimhir, an Codán agus an Uimhir Dheachúlach.
:9-1-1-p_en: The main versions of the number in Irish are the Bare Number, the Cardinal Number, the Personal Number, the Ordinal Number, the Fraction and the Decimal Number.

:9-1-1-a-title_ga: An Mhaoluimhir
:9-1-1-a-title_en: The Bare Number

:9-1-1-a-p_ga: Tugtar maoluimhir ar uimhir nach dtagann ainmfhocal go díreach ar a lorg. Úsáidtear an mhaoluimhir i gcomhaireamh lom, e.g., _a haon_; _a cúig_; _a deich_ (féach 9.2).
:9-1-1-a-p_en: A number that is not directly followed by a noun is called a "bare number". The bare number is used in counting, e.g., _a haon_; _a cúig_; _a deich_ (see 9.2).

:9-1-1-b-title_ga: An Bhunuimhir
:9-1-1-b-title_en: The Cardinal Number

:9-1-1-b-p_ga: Tugtar bunuimhir ar uimhir a chuirtear roimh ainmfhocal chun líon rudaí a chur in iúl, e.g., _trí leabhar_; _seacht gcupán_; _naoi mbád_ (féach 9.3).
:9-1-1-b-p_en: A number that is placed before a noun to express a number of things is called a "cardinal number", e.g., _trí leabhar_; _seacht gcupán_; _naoi mbád_ (see 9.3).

:9-1-1-c-title_ga: An Uimhir Phearsanta
:9-1-1-c-title_en: The Personal Number

:9-1-1-c-p_ga: Tugtar uimhir phearsanta ar uimhir a úsáidtear chun daoine a chomhaireamh, e.g., _beirt bhan_; _ceathrar daltaí_; _ochtar feirmeoirí_ (féach 9.4).
:9-1-1-c-p_en: A number that is used to count people is called a "personal number", e.g., _beirt bhan_; _ceathrar daltaí_; _ochtar feirmeoirí_ (see 9.4).

:9-1-1-d-title_ga: An Orduimhir
:9-1-1-d-title_en: The Ordinal Number

:9-1-1-d-p_ga: Tugtar orduimhir ar uimhir lena gcuirtear ionad ruda i sraith rudaí in iúl, e.g., _an chéad teach_; _an dara bean_; _an fichiú haois_ (féach 9.5).
:9-1-1-d-p_en: A number indicating the position of an object in a series of objects is called an "ordinal number", e.g., _an chéad teach_; _an dara bean_; _an fichiú haois_ (see 9.5).

:9-1-1-e-title_ga: An Codán
:9-1-1-e-title_en: The Fraction

:9-1-1-e-p_ga: Cainníocht uimhriúil nach slánuimhir (uimhir iomlán) is ea an codán, e.g., _ceathrú (1/4)_; _ceithre chúigiú (4/5)_; _cúig shéú (5/6)_ (féach 9.6).
:9-1-1-e-p_en: A "fraction" is a numerical quantity that is not an integer (whole number)., e.g., _ceathrú (1/4)_; _ceithre chúigiú (4/5)_; _cúig shéú (5/6)_ (see 9.6).

:9-1-1-f-title_ga: An Uimhir Dheachúlach
:9-1-1-f-title_en: The Decimal Number

:9-1-1-f-p_ga: Bealach eile chun cainníocht uimhriúil nach slánuimhir a chur in iúl, agus úsáid á baint as an bpointe deachúlach, is ea an uimhir dheachúlach, e.g., _3.1_; _7.5_; _18.49_; _34.023_ (féach 9.7).
:9-1-1-f-p_en: The "decimal number" is another way of expressing a non-integer numerical quantity, using the decimal point, e.g., _3.1_; _7.5_; _18.49_; _34.023_ (see 9.7).

:9-1-2-p_ga: Tugtar rogha idir dhá chóras chun déileáil le bunuimhreacha agus le huimhreacha pearsanta sa scríbhneoireacht: an Córas Seanbhunaithe agus an Córas Simplithe. Úsáidtear an Córas Seanbhunaithe le huimhreacha ó 1-19 i gcónaí. Is féidir an Córas Seanbhunaithe nó an Córas Simplithe a úsáid le huimhreacha ó 20 ar aghaidh.
:9-1-2-p_en: A choice is given between two systems for dealing with cardinal numbers and personal numbers in writing: the Established System and the Simplified System. Úsáidtear an Córas Seanbhunaithe le huimhreacha ó 1-19 i gcónaí. Is féidir an Córas Seanbhunaithe nó an Córas Simplithe a úsáid le huimhreacha ó 20 ar aghaidh.

:9-2-title_ga: An Mhaoluimhir
:9-2-title_en: The Bare Number

:9-2-p_ga: Tugtar maoluimhir ar uimhir a úsáidtear i gcomhaireamh lom nuair nach gcuirtear líon nó ord rudaí nó daoine in iúl, e.g., _a haon_; _a trí déag_; _a tríocha trí_; _céad nócha a cúig_; _míle, ceithre chéad seachtó a naoi_.
:9-2-p_en: A bare number is a number used in counting when the number or order of things or people is not indicated, e.g., _a haon_; _a trí déag_; _a tríocha trí_; _céad nócha a cúig_; _míle, ceithre chéad seachtó a naoi_.

:9-2-1-title_ga: Na Maoluimhreacha 0-99
:9-2-1-title_en: The Bare Numbers 0-99

:9-2-1-a-p_ga: Cuirtear an mhír _a_ roimh uimhreacha ó 1-19, e.g., _a haon_; _a dó_; _a trí_; _bus a ceathair_; _a hocht déag_; agus i gcás 20-99 (seachas na deicheanna), cuirtear í roimh an uimhir dheiridh, e.g., _fiche a haon_; _daichead a sé_; _seachtó a hocht_.
:9-2-1-a-p_en: The particle _a_ is placed before numbers from 1-19, e.g., _a haon_; _a dó_; _a trí_; _bus a ceathair_; _a hocht déag_; and for 20-99 (other than the tens), it is placed before the last number, e.g., _fiche a haon_; _daichead a sé_; _seachtó a hocht_.

:9-2-1-b-p_ga: Tá comhstádas ag na focail _ceathracha_ agus _daichead_.
:9-2-1-b-p_en: The words _ceathracha_ and _daichead_ have a similar status. (_Note: Both of these mean "forty."_)

:9-2-1-p1_ga: Seo mar a scríobhtar na maoluimhreacha ó 0-19.
:9-2-1-p1_en: This is how the bare numbers from 0-19 are written.

:TheBareNumbers_ga: Na Maoluimhreacha
:TheBareNumbers_en: The Bare Numbers

:9-2-1-p2_ga: Tabhair faoi deara go n-úsáidtear an mhaoluimhir i gcúrsaí ama, e.g., _a haon a chlog_; _a seacht a chlog_; _a fiche tar éis a cúig_. Seo mar a scríobhtar an t-am i bhfoirm figiúirí: _5.30 p.m._; _4.26 p.m._; _13:00_; _16:47_.
:9-2-1-p2_en: Note that the bare number is used for times of day, e.g., _a haon a chlog_; _a seacht a chlog_; _a fiche tar éis a cúig_. This is how time is written in the form of figures: _5.30 p.m._; _4.26 p.m._; _13:00_; _16:47_.

:9-2-1-p3_ga: Seo mar a scríobhtar na maoluimhreacha ó 20-99. Tugtar an córas iomlán idir 20 agus 29 sa tábla agus tugtar samplaí i gcás na ndeicheanna eile.
:9-2-1-p3_en: This is how the bare numbers from 20-99 are written. The entire system between 20 and 29 is given in the table and examples are given for the other tens.

:9-2-2-title_ga: Na Maoluimhreacha 100+
:9-2-2-title_en: The Bare Numbers 100+

:9-2-2-a-p_ga: Is ainmfhocail iad na huimhreacha _céad_, _míle_, _milliún_, _billiún_, etc. Dá bhrí sin, is é córas na mbunuimhreacha a úsáidtear má tá na huimhreacha 2-19 roimh shraith náideanna a fhreagraíonn do _céad_, _míle_, _milliún_, _billiún_, etc., e.g., _ceithre chéad_; _dhá chéad míle_; _naoi milliún déag_; _dhá bhilliún_; _ocht mbilliún_ (féach 9.3 An Bhunuimhir).
:9-2-2-a-p_en: The numbers _céad_, _míle_, _milliún_, _billiún_, etc. are nouns. Therefore, the cardinal number system is used if the numbers 2-19 are followed by a series of zeros corresponding to _céad_, _míle_, _milliún_, _billiún_, etc., e.g., _ceithre chéad_; _dhá chéad míle_; _naoi milliún déag_; _dhá bhilliún_; _ocht mbilliún_ (see 9.3 The Cardinal Number).

:9-2-2-b-p_ga: Ní dhéantar athrú tosaigh ar an ainmfhocal i ndiaidh na ndeicheanna, e.g., _fiche míle_; _tríocha milliún_; etc.
:9-2-2-b-p_en: No initial mutation is made to the noun after the tens, e.g., _fiche míle_; _tríocha milliún_; etc.

:9-2-3-title_ga: Na Maoluimhreacha 10,000+
:9-2-3-title_en: The Bare Numbers 10,000+

:9-2-3-a-p_ga: Tá dhá chóras ann chun na maoluimhreacha os cionn 10,000 a chur in iúl -- córas na mbunuimhreacha agus córas na maoluimhreacha.
:9-2-3-a-p_en: There are two systems for expressing odd numbers beyond 10,000 -- the cardinal number system and the bare number system.

:9-2-3-b-p_ga: I gcóras na mbunuimhreacha leanann ord na bhfocal rialacha na mbunuimhreacha, e.g., _24,000 -- ceithre mhíle is fiche_ (féach tábla 9N). Faoi chóras na maoluimhreacha, úsáidtear an mhaoluimhir móide an t-ainmfhocal lom (_céad_, _míle_, etc.) gan chlaochlú, e.g., _24,000 -- fiche a ceathair míle_.
:9-2-3-b-p_en: In the cardinal number system the word order follows the cardinal number rules, e.g., _24,000 -- ceithre mhíle is fiche_ (see table 9N). Under the bare number system, the bare number plus the bare noun is used (_céad_, _míle_, etc.) without transformation, e.g., _24,000 -- fiche a ceathair míle_.

:9-2-3-p1_ga: Tabhair faoi deara go n-úsáidtear _is_ i gcóras na mbunuimhreacha os cionn 10,000 ach nach n-úsáidtear é sna maoluimhreacha eile.
:9-2-3-p1_en: Note that _is_ is used in the cardinal number system above 10,000 but not in the other bare numbers.

:9-2-3-p2_ga: Seo mar a scríobhtar na maoluimhreacha os cionn 10,000.
:9-2-3-p2_en: This is how the odd numbers over 10,000 are written.

:9-3-title_ga: An Bhunuimhir
:9-3-title_en: The Cardinal Number

:9-3-1-title_ga: Na Bunuimhreacha 1-19
:9-3-1-title_en: The Cardinal Numbers 1-19

:9-3-1-a-p_ga: Tugtar _bunuimhir_ ar an uimhir a chuirtear le hainmfhocal chun líon rudaí a chur in iúl.
:9-3-1-a-p_en: The number added to a noun to express the number of things is called a _cardinal number_.

:9-3-1-b-p_ga: Tagann an t-ainmfhocal ar lorg _aon_, _dhá_, _trí_, _ceithre_, _cúig_, _sé_, _seacht_, _ocht_, _naoi_, _deich_.
:9-3-1-b-p_en: The noun comes after _aon_, _dhá_, _trí_, _ceithre_, _cúig_, _sé_, _seacht_, _ocht_, _naoi_, _deich_.

:9-3-1-c-p_ga: Tabhair faoi deara go n-úsáidtear na bunuimhreacha _dhá_ agus _ceithre_ seachas na foirmeacha maoluimhreach _dó_ agus _ceathair_.
:9-3-1-c-p_en: Note that the cardinal numbers _dhá_ and _ceithre_ are used rather than the diminutive forms _dó_ and _ceathair_.

:9-3-1-d-p_ga: Ní mór an focal _amháin_ a lua tar éis na bunuimhreach _aon_ agus an ainmfhocail. Ní gá, áfach, _aon_ a lua i gcónaí, e.g., _aon chapall amháin_; _capall amháin_.
:9-3-1-d-p_en: The word _amháin_ must be mentioned after the original _aon_ and the noun. It is not, however, always necessary to mention _aon_, e.g., _aon chapall amháin_; _capall amháin_.

:9-3-1-e-p_ga: Ní chuirtear séimhiú ar _d_, _t_ nó _s_ tar éis _aon_, e.g., _aon doras amháin_; _aon teach amháin_.
:9-3-1-e-p_en: _d_, _t_ or _s_ is not lenited after _aon_, e.g., _aon doras amháin_; _aon teach amháin_.

:9-3-1-f-p_ga: I ndiaidh an ainmfhocail a thagann an focal _déag_, e.g., _ceithre leabhar déag_. Séimhítear an focal _déag_ má chríochnaíonn an focal díreach roimhe ar ghuta (ach féach 9.3.2(e)), seachas i ndiaidh _euro_.
:9-3-1-f-p_en: After the noun comes the word _déag_, e.g., _ceithre leabhar déag_. The word _déag_ is omitted if the word immediately preceding it ends in a vowel (but see 9.3.2(e)), rather than after _euro_.

:9-3-1-p_ga: Tabhair faoi deara, más aonad brí ar leith an t-ainmfhocal agus an cáilitheoir, e.g., _madra rua_; _cat crainn_; _teach tábhairne_; _láthair champála_ (seachas gnáth-ainmfhocal agus cáilitheoir, e.g., _leabhar dearg_) is i ndiaidh an ainmfhocail agus an cháilitheora a chuirtear _déag_, e.g., _ceithre mhadra rua dhéag_; _cúig theach tábhairne dhéag_; _sé chat crainn déag_; _seacht n-iora ghlasa dhéag_. Cuirtear aon cháilitheoir eile i ndiaidh _déag_, e.g., _sé chat crainn déag mhóra_; _seacht n-iora ghlasa dhéag fhiáine_.
:9-3-1-p_en: Note that if the noun and the qualifier are a separate unit of meaning, e.g., _madra rua_ (_EN: fox_); _cat crainn_ (_EN: pine marten_); _teach tábhairne_ (_EN: pub_); _láthair champála_ (_EN: campsite_) (rather than an ordinary noun and qualifier, e.g., _leabhar dearg_) _déag_ is placed after the noun and the qualifier, e.g., _ceithre mhadra rua dhéag_; _cúig theach tábhairne dhéag_; _sé chat crainn déag_; _seacht n-iora ghlasa dhéag_. Any other qualifier is added after _déag_, e.g., _sé chat crainn déag mhóra_; _seacht n-iora ghlasa dhéag fhiáine_.

:9-3-1-g-p_ga: Ní shéimhítear _dhá_ i ndiaidh an ailt, _aon_ ná _céad_, e.g., _an dá leabhar_; _an chéad dá leabhar_; _aon dá leabhar_.
:9-3-1-g-p_en: _two_ is not lenited after the article, _aon_ or _céad_, e.g., _an dá leabhar_; _an chéad dá leabhar_; _aon dá leabhar_.

:9-3-1-h-p_ga: Cuireann na bunuimhreacha _aon_, _dhá_ (ach féach 8.3.3), _trí_, _ceithre_, _cúig_ agus _sé_ séimhiú ar an ainmfhocal uatha a thagann ina ndiaidh, e.g., _trí bhád_; ach ní shéimhítear an focal _cent_, e.g., _trí cent_.
:9-3-1-h-p_en: The cardinal numbers _aon_, _dhá_ (but see 8.3.3), _trí_, _ceithre_, _cúig_ and _sé_ lenite the following singular noun, e.g., _trí bhád_; but the word _cent_ is not lenited, e.g., _trí cent_.

:9-3-1-i-p_ga: Cuireann na bunuimhreacha _seacht_, _ocht_, _naoi_ agus _deich_ urú ar an ainmfhocal uatha agus iolra a thagann ina ndiaidh, e.g., _seacht gcnámh_; _deich gcinn_; ach ní uraítear an focal _euro_, e.g., _ocht euro_; _naoi euro_, ná _cent_, e.g., _ocht cent_; _deich cent_.
:9-3-1-i-p_en: The cardinal numbers _seacht_, _ocht_, _naoi_ and _deich_ eclipse the following singular and plural nouns, e.g., _seacht gcnámh_; _deich gcinn_; but the word _euro_ is not eclipsed, e.g., _ocht euro_; _naoi euro_, and neither is _cent_, e.g., _ocht cent_; _deich cent_.

:9-3-2-a-p_ga: Foirm an ainmnigh uatha de na hainmfhocail a úsáidtear leis na bunuimhreacha 1-2 agus i ndiaidh uimhreacha a chríochnaíonn ar 1-2, e.g., _aon phingin amháin_; _dhá bhliain_; _aon uair déag_; _dhá cheann is tríocha_.
:9-3-2-a-p_en: The nominative singular form of nouns used with the base numbers 1-2 and after numbers ending in 1-2, e.g., _aon phingin amháin_; _dhá bhliain_; _aon uair déag_; _dhá cheann is tríocha_.

:9-3-2-b-p_ga: Ní chuirtear séimhiú ar an leagan iolra i ndiaidh 3-6 ná i ndiaidh uimhreacha a chríochnaíonn ar 3-6, e.g., _trí seachtaine_; _ceithre cinn déag_.
:9-3-2-b-p_en: The plural form is not lenited after 3-6 or after numbers ending in 3-6, e.g., _trí seachtaine_; _ceithre cinn déag_.

:9-3-2-c-p_ga: I ndiaidh 3-6 agus i ndiaidh uimhreacha a chríochnaíonn ar 3-6 cuirtear _h_ roimh an leagan iolra _uaire_, e.g., _cúig huaire_.
:9-3-2-c-p_en: After 3-6 and after numbers ending in 3-6 _h_ is preceded by the plural form _uaire_, e.g., _cúig huaire_.

:9-3-2-d-p_ga: Cuireann na huimhreacha 7-10 agus uimhreacha a chríochnaíonn ar 7-9 urú ar an leagan iolra, e.g., _seacht mbliana_; _ocht bpingine déag_.
:9-3-2-d-p_en: The numbers 7-10 and numbers ending in 7-9 eclipse the plural form, e.g., _seacht mbliana_; _ocht bpingine déag_.

:9-3-2-e-p_ga: Ní chuirtear séimhiú ar _déag_ i ndiaidh an leagain iolra, fiú amháin sa chás go gcríochnaíonn sé ar ghuta, e.g., _trí bliana déag_; _ocht n-uaire déag_.
:9-3-2-e-p_en: _déag_ is not lenited after the plural form, even if it ends in a vowel, e.g., _trí bliana déag_; _ocht n-uaire déag_.

:9-3-2-f-p_ga: Is í foirm an ainmnigh uatha a úsáidtear i ndiaidh na ndeicheanna (seachas _deich_), e.g., _fiche bliain_; _tríocha pingin_; _ochtó troigh_.
:9-3-2-f-p_en: The nominative singular form is used after the tens (other than _deich_), e.g., _fiche bliain_; _tríocha pingin_; _ochtó troigh_.
