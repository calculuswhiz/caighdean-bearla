:7-1-title_ga: Ginearálta
:7-1-title_en: General

:7-1-p1_ga: Úsáidtear an chopail chun dhá fhocal nó dhá abairtín a cheangal le chéile.
:7-1-p1_en: The copula is used to associate two words or phrases together.

:7-1-p2_ga: Is ionann an leagan den chopail a úsáidtear san aimsir láithreach agus an leagan a úsáidtear san aimsir fháistineach. Ina theannta sin, is ionann an leagan den chopail a úsáidtear san aimsir chaite agus an leagan a úsáidtear san aimsir ghnáthchaite agus sa mhodh coinníollach. Tabhair faoi deara, más san aimsir chaite, san aimsir ghnáthchaite nó sa mhodh coinníollach atá faisnéis na habairte, nach gá go mbeadh aimsir na copaile agus aimsir fhaisnéis na habairte ag aontú le chéile. Féach 7.2 le haghaidh fhoirmeacha simplí na copaile.
:7-1-p2_en: The form of the copula used in the present tense is the same as that used in the future tense. Moreover, the form of the copula, when used in the past tense, the past habitual tense, and the conditional mood, is the same. Bear in mind, if the predicate of the sentence is in the past tense, the present tense, or the conditional mood, the tense of the copula and the tense of the predicate would not necessarily agree with each other. See 7.2 for simple forms of the copula.

:7-2-title.ga: Foirmeacha Simplí na Copaile agus Athruithe Tosaigh

:7-2-a-p_ga: Is ionann foirm shimplí agus foirm den chopail nach bhfuil i gcumasc le forainmneach ceisteach, réamhfhocal nó cónasc.
:7-2-a-p_en: A simple form is a form of the copula that is not combined with an interrogative pronoun, preposition or conjunction.

:7-2-b-p_ga: Leagtar amach anseo foirmeacha simplí na copaile:
:7-2-b-p_en: The simple forms of copula are set out here:

:7-2-b-i-p_ga: i bpríomhchlásal;
:7-2-b-i-p_en: in a main clause;

:7-2-b-ii-p_ga: i dtús fochlásail, seachas clásal coibhneasta;
:7-2-b-ii-p_en: at the beginning of a subordinate clause, rather than a relative clause;

:7-2-b-iii-p_ga: i gclásal coibhneasta díreach;
:7-2-b-iii-p_en: in a direct relative clause;

:7-2-b-iv-p_ga: i gclásal coibhneasta indíreach.
:7-2-b-iv-p_en: in an indirect relative clause.

:7-2-b-p2_ga: Déantar na foirmeacha a scagadh de réir aimsire. I ngach cás, tugtar an aimsir láithreach agus an aimsir fháistineach ar dtús agus tugtar an aimsir chaite, an aimsir ghnáthchaite agus an modh coinníollach ina dhiaidh sin.
:7-2-b-p2_en: The forms are refined by their tense. In each situation, the present tense and the future tense are given first followed by the past tense, the past habitual tense and the conditional mood.

:7-2-1-B-title_ga: An Aimsir Chaite, an Aimsir Ghnáthchaite agus an Modh Coinníollach
:7-2-1-B-title_en: The Past Tense, the Past Habitual Tense and the Conditional Mood

:7-2-B-p_ga: Leanann séimhiú foirmeacha na copaile san aimsir chaite, san aimsir ghnáthchaite agus sa mhodh coinníollach. Úsáidtear _ba_ nó _b'_ san aimsir chaite, san aimsir ghnáthchaite nó sa mhodh coinníollach mar seo a leanas:
:7-2-B-p_en: Lenition follows with the forms of the copula in the past tense, the past habitual tense and the conditional mood. _ba_ or _b'_ is used in the past tense, past habitual tense or conditional mood as follows:

:7-2-B-a-p_ga: Cuirtear _ba_ roimh chonsan nó roimh _fh-_ + _consan_ i ngach cás, e.g., _Ba mhúinteoir lách é_; _Ba fhreagra cruinn é_.
:7-2-B-a-p_en: _ba_ is always placed before _fh-_ + _consonant_, e.g., _Ba mhúinteoir lách é_; _Ba fhreagra cruinn é_.

:7-2-B-b-p1_ga: Cuirtear _ba_ roimh ghuta nó roimh _fh-_ + _guta_ sna cásanna seo a leanas:
:7-2-B-b-p1_en: _ba_ is placed before _fh-_ + _vowel_ in the following situations:

:7-2-B-b-i-p_ga: na forainmnigh _é_, _í_, _ea_, _éard_, _eisean_, _ise_, _iad_ agus _iadsan_, e.g., _Ba iad a bhuaigh an corn_;
:7-2-B-b-i-p_en: the forms _é_, _í_, _ea_, _éard_, _eisean_, _ise_, _iad_ and _iadsan_, e.g., _Ba iad a bhuaigh an corn_;

:7-2-B-b-ii-p_ga: réamhfhocail, e.g., _Ba ar theacht i bhfeidhm an Achta a tharla sé_;
:7-2-B-b-ii-p_en: prepositions, e.g., _Ba ar theacht i bhfeidhm an Achta a tharla sé_;

:7-2-B-b-iii-p_ga: forainmnigh réamhfhoclacha, e.g., _Ba ann a bhí sé_;
:7-2-B-b-iii-p_en: prepositional pronouns, e.g., _Ba ann a bhí sé_;

:7-2-B-b-iv-p_ga: dobhriathra nach dobhriathra aidiachtacha iad, e.g., _Ba arú inné a d'ith siad an cáca_.
:7-2-B-b-iv-p_en: adverbs that are not adjectival adverbs, e.g., _Ba arú inné a d'ith siad an cáca_.

:7-2-B-c-p_ga: Cuirtear _b'_ roimh ghuta nó roimh _fh-_ + _guta_ sna cásanna seo a leanas:
:7-2-B-c-p_en: _b'_ is placed before a vowel or before _fh-_ + _vowel_ in the following cases:

:7-2-B-c-i-p_ga: ainmfhocail, e.g., _B'innealtóir maith í_;
:7-2-B-c-i-p_en: nouns, e.g., _B'innealtóir maith í_;

:7-2-B-c-ii-p_ga: aidiachtaí, e.g., _B'fhearúil an duine é_;
:7-2-B-c-ii-p_en: adjectives, e.g., _B'fhearúil an duine é_;

:7-2-B-c-iii-p_ga: na forainmnigh thaispeántacha _eo_, _in_ agus _iúd_, e.g., _B'in é é_; _B'eo amach é_.
:7-2-B-c-iii-p_en: the demonstrative pronouns _eo_, _in_ and _iúd_, e.g., _B'in é é_; _B'eo amach é_.

:7-2-B-c-iv-p_ga: dobhriathra aidiachtacha, e.g., _B'annamh a dhéanadh sé é_.
:7-2-B-c-iv-p_en: adjectival adverbs, e.g., _B'annamh a dhéanadh sé é_.

:7-2-B-d-p_ga: Ceadaítear na foirmeacha _ba_ nó _ab_ a úsáid roimh an bhforainmneach neodrach _ea_ i lár abairt atá san aimsir chaite, san aimsir ghnáthchaite nó sa mhodh coinníollach, e.g., _Saighdiúirí ab ea iad_ nó _Saighdiúirí ba ea iad_.
:7-2-B-d-p_en: The forms _ba_ or _ab_ are allowed to be used before the neuter pronoun _ea_ in the middle of a sentence that is in the past, present or conditional tenses, e.g., _Saighdiúirí ab ea iad_ nó _Saighdiúirí ba ea iad_.
